Junior Program

The program is designed for ages 2-6 to aid in the development, coordination and socialization of your skater. Bring your skates or get used to the ice in your favorite pair of snow boots.

Teenager Program

For ages 11 to 17. Previous skating experience is recommended. This is a skills-based class designed for players with little to no hockey experience that will focus on developing basic hockey fundamentals and introducing game concepts.

Many professional hockey teams have structured programs for developing young players and preparing them for the NHL. These programs typically involve training camps, tournaments, and assignments to minor league teams.
